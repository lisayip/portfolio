Projects

DECODE

“From April to June 2019, an estimated 1.33 million people were unemployed in UK”

“Rising unemployment is linked to social costs to local and regional areas and higher crime rates.”

Technology is fundamentally changing the landscape in which we work, live and play. Having a key hub which helps to keep abreast of new knowledge and technology is vital to staying current in your work.

Research

I set out to identify the growing number of unemployment in the UK, the impact it has on the social and economic society and the future of work. My aim was to improve the method of channels and streamline the recruitment process and to reduce barriers people face looking for work.

Challenges

Many problems users faced were the lack of personal connection between companies and users along the recruitment process, the repetitive and arduous task of looking for suitable job openings and long lag time in getting interviews. Designing a knowledge hub bringing tech and companies together will be the initial step in bridging the social and unemployement gap in communities and bring work, life balance into play.

Solutions

  • Enhanced filter system to search for jobs that match your requirements.
  • Favourite and track your applications.
  • Upload personal videos to attract the right employer.
  • Bridge skill gaps and build strong community and network ties.
