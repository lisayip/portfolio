Services

I concept and craft user journeys that bring delight to people and solve complicated problems.

Research

I take a human centred design approach to establish the existing ground first. This starts with asking the right questions, to market research and competitive analysis to conducting interviews and survey to ensure we are designing to meet the challenges.

Design

Whether designing for physical, digital or mobile it’s important to ensure your designs meet the needs of users via prototypes which simulate the final end results and tested with users to ensure they are useable and useful before development.

Build

This is the stage which I can bring the prototypes and sketches to life with front end development or work closely with a team of developers to flesh out the final product. It is important to build out the designs to test with users for usability issues.
