I participated in a Visa design hack in which our team won second place. Our aim was to design an application suitable for small to medium sized enterprises to help ease their day to day business banking.

Challenges

An issue consistently highlighted through research was how consumers still did not feel banking was safe enough. Ensuring safer daily transactions for businesses which allowed consumers to pay for goods online and via phone was our challenge as we conducted research through the local business consumers, ideated, created userflows, wireframes and prototyped.

Solutions

  • Single use card generator which included digital displays on a physical card
  • Eliminate card cloning and safer transactions locally and abroad
  • Ability to have mulitple cards for employees and contractors
  • Comfort in sharing card details via phone
