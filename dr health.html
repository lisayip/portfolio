DR. HEALTH

Health professionals and users need access to patient’s health data to make informed decisions quickly and simply. This app was designed to centralise patient’s data and shift health information direct to the practitioner and patients hands. Users can book appointments, search for information, chat and speak to doctors and order medicine online.

Research

Since the beginning of the health pandemic, getting access to health services have been disrupted from the way we consult with doctors to the way we order medicine. I conducted research and interviews with people and determined a few underlying issues which stemmed from the sudden change in the way we now access our health services. Not being able to see a doctor during the lockdown, to ordering repeat prescriptions and having no centralised access to your health data were a few main issues uncovered. Designing to resolve some of the basic problems people face during this period was the main priority to finding better solutions to healthcare access.

Challenges

  • Difficulties in ordering medications.
  • Not being able to see a doctor and difficulties in accessing health services.
  • No centralised access to patient’s records for users.

Solutions

  • Having knowledge and latest health information.
  • Being able to see doctor’s availability and book appointments simply.
  • Arranging to chat or consult with a doctor easily.
  • Chat to Dr. Bot and help self diagnose to gain a better understanding of your illness.
  • Keeping track of patient’s medical history.
  • Order medicine and repeat prescriptions online.
